Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso®)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

How to make the goddamn thing:

  1. Step 1: Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  2. Step 2: Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Step 4: Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
